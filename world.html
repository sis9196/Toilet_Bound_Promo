Mystery No.1

The Clock Keepers

...Have you heard the rumor?

It's school mystery number one - The three clock keepers

There's a big, big old clock somwhere in the school.

That clock is time itself - the time that flows through the school.

If you move the hands of that clock, the school's time is yours to command.

But you see...

...There are guards watching over the big clock.

They are the three clock keepers who represent pats, present, and future.

If anyone touches the clock without their permission...

...The three clock keepers will steal the person's life...!

The three Clock Keepers are the first of the Seven Mysteries. They are the first school mystery to be seen independently outside of their boundary and unrelated to the rumor. Unlike the other school mysteries, the clock keepers consist of three members - Kako, Mirai, and Akane. They represent past, future, and present. The First School Mystery oversees the "Time" aspect of Kamome Academy. Their boundary is the Big Clock at the Sealed Auditorium and if the Big Clock is destroyed, the entire academy's time will be stoped and will open the Clock Keepers Boundary.

Mystery No.2

The Misaki Stairs

...Have you heard?

There is a rule regarding that staircase that you must never break.

You must never set foot on the fourth set. Why? Because...

...you will be taken to the other side?

The Misaki Stair is the second of the Seven Mysteries. The Second School Mystery oversees the "Space" aspect of Kamome Academy. The boundary has a long winding stairway extending to the sky through shrine gates and buildings. The innermost part of the boundary is actually at the bottom of stairs, which can be accessed by jumping off the edge of the boundary and being carried by the water currents.Yako is a supernatural that is the current representative of the Second School Mystery.

Hell of Mirrors

...Did you know?

No.3 of the school's seven mysteryes...

...Is the Hell of Mirrors

It's a wonderland on the other side of the looking glass.

There are mirrors there that reflect...

...The hearts of all those who wander inside.

Somoene with a beautiful or brave heart will be just fine.

They'll be able to return to their world without any trouble.

The Hell of Mirrors is the third of the Seven Mysteries. The third Mystery's representative has the ability to reflect the hearts of those who wandered inside its boundary. The Hell of Mirror's is an expansive boundary, mutileveled space, with many mirrors decorating the walls. The mirror will change its reflection based on the person's deepest secrets. If the person is of pure heart, they will be able to leave easily, but if there is an ounce of fear in someone, the mirror will reflect it to beckon the victim to stay forver. The current representative of the Hell of Mirrors is Mistuba Sousuke after being forced to resurrection by eating the previous Mystery No.3.

Mystery No.6

God of Death

School Mystery No.6 - God of Death.

On the night of tsukimi,

The Reaper is rumored to steal the lives of those who fall asleep while waiting to view the moon.

And can be heard playing a melody on the flute by individuals who will die soon.

As a result, people weave baskets all night to stay awake and protect those who hear the melody.

The God of Death is the sixth of the Seven Mysteries.The sixth mystery begins with the event called "Tsukimachu" where children gather together weaving baskets all night until sunrise. The children have to stay awake the whole night or their souls will attract The God of Death. The Sixth School Mystery oversees in Kamome Academy is life and death. The current representative is Shinigama-Sama a supernatural known as the Reaper and guards the boundary closest to the Far Shore and can only appear once each year for the event. The boundary of No.6 is under the school. The boundary itself has water as its floor and is connected by series of bridges. This boundary is larger than all the other boundaries.

She will grant one wish to anyone who summons her spirit.

To summon the ghost, knock three times...

"Hanako-san Hanako-san."

Hanako-San of the Toilet is the seventh of the Seven Mysteries. In popular urban legends, it is assumed that Hanako-san is a female as it haunts the girls bathroom, but there is no gender indication. As similar to the urban legend, calling her name would cause her to appear and reply back. Some say that Hanako-san would grant a favour for the person who called out for her at a price. Hanako-san is the leader of the Seven Mysteries and maintains the balance between the humans and supernaturals at the school. His boundary is said to be within the girl's bathroom.
